Tips & tricks

Connect

  • Type channel names, press Enter or Connect
  • The red ✕ disconnects that platform
  • Your channels are saved — and live in the page URL

Popout & sharing

  • Popout opens the chat in its own window
  • The popout link works on its own — bookmark it, make a desktop shortcut, or share it
  • Install the popout as an app (browser address bar) — it opens with your last used channels

Watch the stream

  • In the popout, press ▶ (top right) — starts muted
  • Click the platform icons to switch source
  • YouTube appears once the channel is live

Alert sounds

  • Settings → Alert sounds: paste your alert overlay URL (StreamElements etc.) — its sound plays in the popout
  • Click 🔊 Enable alert sound once per popout window (browser rule)
  • Alert URLs hold your secret token — remove &alerts= before sharing a popout link

OBS overlay

  • Swap /popout for /overlay in the link and add it as a Browser source
  • Transparent background — chat floats right on the stream image
  • Messages fade out after 60 s; &fade=120 changes the lifetime
  • Tune it: &size=20 (text px), &align=right, &max=10 (message count), &icons=0 (hide platform icons)

Good to know

  • Twitch badges and 7TV/BTTV/FFZ emotes show automatically — toggle them in Settings
  • Messages that @-mention a connected channel are highlighted
  • Filter chips hide platforms in your view only
  • Deleted messages stay, with a strikethrough
  • The feed keeps the latest 200 messages
